--- layout: default title: Home --- # HE Climate Perspectives 🌍 This UCL Grand Challenge – Climate Crisis project aims to gain a better understanding of students’ perspectives on how Higher Education can better prepare students by exploring their expectations, values and beliefs. We adopted a **participatory co-production** approach to research; student partners are involved at all stages of the research process - from research design to data collection, analysis and the dissemination of findings and recommendations.
